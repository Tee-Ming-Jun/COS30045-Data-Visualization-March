About Us

Who We Are

This website was developed as part of an academic project to raise awareness about household appliance energy consumption. It focuses on helping users understand how everyday appliances, such as televisions, impact electricity usage.

Our Goal

Our goal is to provide simple and accessible information that allows users to make smarter decisions when choosing appliances. By understanding energy usage, users can reduce electricity costs and contribute to a more sustainable environment.

What We Provide

  • 📊 Realistic appliance energy data
  • 📺 Television power consumption insights
  • ⚡ Easy-to-read comparison tables
  • 🌱 Awareness of energy efficiency

Future Improvements

In the future, this platform can be expanded to include more appliances, interactive charts, and personalized recommendations for energy-saving solutions.
